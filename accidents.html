Modern day accidents

Client: iNews

From the embarrassment of reading a text and walking into a lamp post to the agony of stepping on an upturned plug, Britons are being tripped up left, right and centre, a new survey published today reveals. The research, carried out by Censuswide for the National Accident Helpline, lays bare the modern menaces facing the nation on a daily basis.
