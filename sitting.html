Sitting is the new cancer

Client: iNews

Millions of people die every year from unhealthy working conditions, at least 1.4 million in Europe alone, according to a new report from the World Health Organisation. The study also expressed concern over “poor work posture and prolonged sitting,” which many British office workers will be familiar with.
