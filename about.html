Who is this
BEUGISM guy?

I am a London based illustrator. I was born and raised in East Berlin. My work sits somewhere between character design and infographics. In my practice I use a healthy mix of both traditional and digital techniques. I mainly draw inspiration from communist murals, Japanese woodcuts and surrealist paintings.
