Desert Island Discs

Client: iNews

Over 3,000 guests have appeared on Desert Island Discs since it was first broadcast on 29 January 1942. Each one chooses eight songs, a book and a luxury item. In the 75 years the show has been on air, the make-up of castaways and their musical choices has become more wide ranging, reflecting the burgeoning of pop culture.
